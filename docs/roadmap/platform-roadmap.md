# Platform Roadmap

## Completed (Current Repository State)
- Multi-tenant clinic core workflows.
- CarePilot campaign/reminder/messaging/lead/webinar modules.
- CarePilot AI Calls foundation + operational hardening (scheduler, retries, failover, webhooks, events, transcript foundation).
- Admin templates, notification settings, integrations status.
- AI orchestration hardening v1 (prompt registry, invocation logs, usage/cost foundation, guardrails foundation, AI Ops page).
- Pharmacy and finance operational pages replacing placeholders.

## Current Maturity
- Strong modular backend with provider SPI boundaries.
- Good tenant/RBAC enforcement footprint.
- Operational telemetry present for CarePilot and AI.
- Some advanced governance and moderation layers are foundational only.

## Near-Term
- Expand AI Ops workflows and prompt lifecycle governance.
- Improve queue observability and alerting for reminders/AI calls.
- Strengthen patient-level consent and opt-out enforcement integration.
- Expand admin integrations actions with safe test/connectivity checks.

## Mid-Term
- Webinar provider integrations (Zoom/Meet/Teams) via adapters.
- Inbound lead/webinar automation pipelines.
- Richer engagement scoring and campaign targeting.
- Workflow engine over AI tool/workflow registry foundations.

## Long-Term AI Roadmap
- Realtime AI calling (STT/TTS orchestration, interruption handling).
- AI receptionist and inbound call automation.
- Agentic automation with approvals and policy guardrails.
- Predictive engagement and clinical operations assistants.
- Unified AI cost/performance optimization and model routing.
